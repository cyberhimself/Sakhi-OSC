Job Advertisement

Various Posts at Sakhi: One Stop Centre, Charaideo

For wide publicity, this is to inform all eligible and interested candidates that the Sakhi One Stop Centre, Charaideo is inviting online applications for several purely temporary and contractual positions. The recruitment is open to candidates who are permanent residents of Assam and citizens of India as per Articles 5 to 9 of the Constitution of India.

Available Posts (Online Application Only)

  1. Centre Administrator
  2. Counsellor
  3. Case Worker
  4. Multi-Purpose Worker
  5. Multi-Purpose Worker

Application Dates

  • Starting Date: 20/11/2025
  • Closing Date: 29/12/2025

Eligibility Criteria (General)

  • Be a citizen of India and a permanent resident of Assam
  • Be between 21 to 38 years of age as on 01/01/2025
  • Age relaxation:
    • 5 years for SC, ST(P), ST(H)
    • As per norms for PwD candidates

Documents to Be Uploaded

Candidates must upload the following documents while submitting their online application:

  1. Filled-in Application Form
  2. Educational Qualification Certificates & Mark Sheets
  3. Age Proof
  4. Voter ID / Residential Proof
  5. Work Experience Certificates (if applicable)
  6. Caste Certificate (if claiming relaxation)
  7. NOC from employer (if currently employed)
  8. Self-attested photograph (as per application form)

Place of Posting

Selected candidates will be posted at:

  • Sakhi One Stop Centre, Charaideo
    (Under Mission Shakti, MoWCD, Government of India)

Selection Process

  • Only shortlisted candidates will be informed
  • Communication will be sent only through email
  • No separate call letters will be issued
  • No TA/DA will be provided for attending the walk-in interview
